## What plans does NovaCRM offer?
NovaCRM offers three plans: Starter ($19/user/month), Business ($49/user/month), and Enterprise ($99/user/month). All plans are billed annually. Monthly billing is available at a 20% premium.

## Is there a free trial?
Yes. NovaCRM offers a 14-day free trial on all plans with no credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial period.

## What's included in the Starter plan?
The Starter plan includes up to 5 users, 10,000 contacts, 1 deal pipeline, email integration, basic reporting, and community support.

## What's included in the Business plan?
The Business plan includes unlimited users, 100,000 contacts, 5 deal pipelines, workflow automation, advanced reporting, API access, sandbox environment, and priority email support.

## What's included in the Enterprise plan?
The Enterprise plan includes everything in Business plus unlimited contacts, unlimited pipelines, custom roles and permissions, SSO/SAML, dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone support, and SLA guarantees.

## Can I change plans at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade at any time and the price difference will be prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Yes. We offer 30% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales@novacrm.example.com to apply.

## Is there a discount for annual billing?
Yes. Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly billing on all plans.

## What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and bank transfers (ACH/wire) for annual plans. PayPal is not currently supported.

## What happens when my trial ends?
Your data is preserved for 30 days after the trial ends. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time during this period. After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted.

## Can I get a refund?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. Contact support@novacrm.example.com within 30 days of your first payment for a full refund.
